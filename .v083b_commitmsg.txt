fix(v0.8.3): split merged saveEntry line that kotlinc refused

CI run 29624834889 (the v0.8.3 tag-triggered build) failed at
compileReleaseKotlin:

  e: AniListClient.kt:509:81 Unexpected tokens
     (use ';' to separate expressions on the same line)

Root cause: in the v0.8.3 saveEntry str_replace, the new
`val conn = openPost(...)` line was placed on the same physical line
as the previous `val body = json.encodeToString(...)`. kotlinc cannot
have two top-level val-declarations on the same line without a `;`
separator, and idiomatic Kotlin doesn't use those — so the compiler
hard-rejected the file.

Fix: split the merged line back into two lines with a blank separator

                val body = json.encodeToString(JsonObject.serializer(), payload)

                val conn = openPost("https://graphql.anilist.co", token)

Verified by full file audit: no other `val x = ... val y = ...`
collapses anywhere in AniListClient.kt. The 3 other call sites
(syncUserList / exchangeCodeForToken / getAiringSchedule) all retain
their proper line breaks.
